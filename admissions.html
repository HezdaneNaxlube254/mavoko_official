Admissions

Admissions are open for January and two terms after interview, with clear requirements and a simple application process.

Admission Period

January intake; and any other time after student passes interview.

Requirements, Documents, Fees

We welcome your child to join our school community any time. To begin the admission process, please ensure you have:

1. Certificate of medical fitness — confirming your child is in good health to participate in school activities.

2. Two passport-size photographs — taken at school while in proper school uniform.

3. Basic necessities — including biro pens, padlocks, and personal items for daily use.

4. Birth certificate — a copy of your child's birth certificate for official records.

5. KCPE/KJSEA result slip — a copy from the previous level.

6. Student ID fee — KES 150 for school identification.

7. Exercise books — seven exercise books for various subjects.

8. Photocopying paper — one ream of photocopying paper (80gsm A4) for school use.

9. School uniform — please confirm exact requirements with the school before making any purchases.

10. Games uniform — for physical education and sports activities.

  • Birth certificate copy
  • KCPE/KJSEA result slip copy
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • 2 passport photos in school uniform
  • Filled admission form
  • Day Scholars: KES 8,250 (confirmed)
  • Boarders: [To be confirmed by bursar]
  • Payment method: Bank transfer (no M-Pesa paybill active — confirm with school).

Admission Checklist

  1. Obtain and fill admission form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Attend interview
  4. Receive admission letter
  5. Pay fees
  6. Report to school
